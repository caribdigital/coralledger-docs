# Compliance

CoralLedger Comply helps you maintain VAT compliance with real-time monitoring and alerts.

## Why Compliance Matters

Non-compliance with Bahamas VAT regulations can result in:
- **Penalties** - Up to 50% of unpaid VAT
- **Interest** - On late payments
- **Audits** - Increased scrutiny from DIR
- **License Issues** - Business license complications

## Compliance Features

### Real-Time Scoring
Your compliance score (A+ to F) reflects:
- Categorization accuracy
- Timely filing history
- Documentation completeness
- Anomaly resolution

### Anomaly Detection
Our system flags potential issues:
- Unusual transaction patterns
- Category inconsistencies
- Missing documentation
- Calculation discrepancies

### Alerts
Receive notifications for:
- Upcoming filing deadlines
- Detected anomalies
- Score changes
- Required actions

## Compliance Best Practices

1. **Import transactions weekly** - Stay current with your data
2. **Review categories monthly** - Ensure accuracy
3. **Address alerts promptly** - Don't let issues accumulate
4. **File on time** - Before the 28th deadline
