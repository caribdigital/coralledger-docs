## Score Grades

| Grade | Score Range | Status |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| A+ | 95-100 | Excellent |
| A | 90-94 | Very Good |
| B | 80-89 | Good |
| C | 70-79 | Fair |
| D | 60-69 | Needs Improvement |
| F | Below 60 | Critical |

## Scoring Factors

Your score is calculated from four weighted components:

### Filing Compliance (30%)
- Returns filed before the 28th deadline
- History of on-time submissions
- No late filing penalties

### Data Quality (25%)
- Completeness of required fields (description, amount, date, VAT rate)
- Consistency of vendor VAT rates and transaction types
- Timeliness of data entry (within 7 days of occurrence)

### Rate Accuracy (25%)
- Correct VAT rate applied to each transaction
- Valid amounts (positive values)
- Transactions within the correct filing period

### Documentation (20%)
- Complete transaction records
- Supporting documentation attached
- Audit trail maintained

## Improving Your Score

### Quick Wins
- Review and confirm pending categorizations
- Resolve any open alerts
- Upload missing documentation

### Long-Term Improvements
- Import transactions more frequently
- Establish consistent categorization rules
- Address root causes of anomalies

## Score Trends

The dashboard shows your score trend over time:
- **Improving** - Score trending upward
- **Stable** - Consistent score maintained
- **Declining** - Score trending downward (action needed)
