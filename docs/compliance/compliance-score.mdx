# Compliance Score

Your compliance score helps you understand your VAT compliance health at a glance.

:::note A factual health indicator — not an attestation
The compliance score is a **factual status indicator** of how your data and filing history look against the platform's checks. It is **not** an attestation that your returns are correct, and it is not a guarantee against assessment or penalty. Treat it as a prompt to review, not a sign-off.
:::

## Score Grades

| Grade | Score Range | Status |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| A+ | 95-100 | Excellent |
| A | 90-94 | Very Good |
| B+ | 85-89 | Good |
| B | 80-84 | Good |
| C | 70-79 | Fair |
| D | 60-69 | Needs Improvement |
| F | Below 60 | Critical |

## Not Yet Assessed

A score renders **only after a real assessment exists**. Until your business clears the activity floor - at least 30 processed transactions AND either a lodged filing on record or 90 days of activity, with a known VAT-registration status - the dashboard and reports show **Not Yet Assessed** instead of a number. A brand-new or sparsely-populated business is never shown a fabricated grade; the Not Yet Assessed card explains what is missing and links to the next step (importing transactions or completing a filing).

## Scoring Factors

Your score is calculated from four weighted components:

### VAT Accuracy (40%)
- Correct VAT rate and arithmetic on each transaction
- Valid amounts within the correct tax period

### Completeness (25%)
- Required fields present on every transaction (date, description, amounts, category)
- No unprocessed gaps in the period

### Consistency (20%)
- Vendor VAT treatment and transaction types applied consistently over time

### Timeliness (15%)
- Returns lodged within the applicable deadline (21 days standard; 14 days for large taxpayers)
- History of on-time lodgement

## When the score is capped

If you have **overdue, unfiled returns**, your overall score is **capped** by filing timeliness regardless of how strong the other factors are — an outstanding statutory obligation is the dominant risk. The dashboard always shows a strip explaining the cap, including the earliest overdue period, so the cap is never silent.

## Transactions pending review

Transactions still awaiting review are **excluded** from the score until they are promoted (VAT-category confirmed and audit-gated). Rather than silently ignoring them, the dashboard surfaces the **pending-review count** with a link to the review queue, so an empty or provisional period is never presented as "all clear".

## Improving Your Score

### Quick Wins
- Review and confirm pending categorizations
- Resolve any open alerts

### Long-Term Improvements
- Import transactions more frequently
- Establish consistent categorization rules
- Address root causes of anomalies

## Score Trends

The dashboard shows your score trend over time:
- **Improving** - Score trending upward
- **Stable** - Consistent score maintained
- **Declining** - Score trending downward (action needed)
