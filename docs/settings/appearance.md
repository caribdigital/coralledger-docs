# Appearance Settings

Customize the visual appearance of CoralLedger Comply to match your preferences.

## Accessing Appearance Settings

Navigate to **Settings > Appearance**.

## Theme Selection

Choose your preferred theme:

| Theme | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Light** | Clean and bright — best for well-lit environments |
| **Dark** | Easy on the eyes — reduces eye strain in low light |
| **System** | Automatically follows your operating system's theme setting |

## Layout Options

### Collapse Sidebar by Default
When enabled, the navigation sidebar starts collapsed, giving you more screen space for content. You can expand it at any time by clicking the menu icon.

### Compact Mode
Reduces spacing and padding throughout the interface for a more information-dense layout. Useful on smaller screens or when you prefer to see more data at once.

## Preview

Changes are previewed immediately as you adjust settings.

## Saving Changes

- Click **Save Appearance Settings** to apply your choices
- Click **Reset to Defaults** to revert to the standard light theme and layout
