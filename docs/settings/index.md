# Settings

Customize CoralLedger Comply to match your business needs.

## Account Settings

### Profile
- Update your name and email
- Change password
- Two-factor authentication setup

### Notifications
Configure when you receive alerts:
- Email notifications
- Dashboard alerts
- Filing reminders

## Business Settings

### Business Profile
- Business name and trading name
- TIN and VAT number
- Business address
- Industry classification

### VAT Configuration
- Filing frequency (monthly/quarterly)
- First VAT period start date
- Food store license status

### Logo and Branding
Upload your logo for:
- VAT invoices
- Generated reports
- PDF exports

## Subscription

### Current Plan
View your subscription details:
- Plan tier
- Billing cycle
- Next payment date

### Upgrade/Downgrade
- Compare plan features
- Change your subscription
- View billing history

### Payment Method
- Update credit card
- PayPal configuration
- Billing address

## Data Management

### Export Data
Export all your data at any time:
- Transaction history (CSV)
- VAT returns (PDF)
- Full data backup

### Data Retention
CoralLedger retains your data for:
- Active accounts: Unlimited
- Closed accounts: 7 years (regulatory requirement)
