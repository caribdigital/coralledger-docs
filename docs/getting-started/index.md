# Quick Start Guide

Welcome to CoralLedger Comply! This guide will help you get started with VAT compliance for your Bahamian business.

:::info Free Beta — March through September 2026
CoralLedger Comply is currently in free beta. All features are available at no cost through September 30, 2026.
:::

## What is CoralLedger Comply?

CoralLedger Comply is a VAT compliance platform built specifically for businesses operating under The Bahamas VAT Act 2014 and the 2025 reforms. It helps you:

- **Record transactions** manually or via CSV import from Point POS and other systems
- **Automatically categorize** transactions by VAT rate (10%, 5%, 0%, exempt)
- **Generate VAT returns** with L1-L31 DIR form fields ready for filing
- **Monitor compliance** with real-time scoring, anomaly detection, and intelligence dashboards
- **Manage multiple clients** through the Firm Portal for accounting professionals

## Getting Started in 5 Minutes

1. **Create your account** — Sign up at [comply.coralledger.com](https://comply.coralledger.com) with email verification and CAPTCHA
2. **Set up 2FA** — Two-factor authentication is required on first login for security
3. **Set up your business** — Enter your TIN (9 digits) and business details
4. **Import transactions** — Upload your CSV or enter transactions manually
5. **Review categorization** — Verify VAT categories are correct
6. **Generate your return** — Create your VAT return with the DIR form format

## Key Features

- **VAT Entry** — Manual and CSV import with multi-rate support and fraud detection
- **VAT Returns** — Draft, submit, and amend returns with full lifecycle tracking
- **Compliance Intelligence** — AI-powered scoring, anomaly detection, and risk assessment
- **Firm Portal** — Multi-client dashboard with batch filing for accounting firms
- **Export Suite** — PDF, XML, Excel, CSV, and Form 301 exports
- **Audit Trail** — Immutable hash-chain verified activity log
- **Security** — 2FA, IP blocking, fraud alerts, and role-based access control
