# Quick Start Guide

Welcome to CoralLedger Comply! This guide will help you get started with VAT compliance for your Bahamian business.

## What is CoralLedger Comply?

CoralLedger Comply is a VAT compliance platform built specifically for businesses operating under The Bahamas VAT Act 2014 and the 2025 reforms. It helps you:

- **Import transactions** from CSV, Excel, or Point POS
- **Automatically categorize** transactions by VAT rate (10%, 5%, 0%, exempt)
- **Generate VAT returns** ready for submission to the Comptroller
- **Monitor compliance** with real-time scoring and alerts

## Getting Started in 5 Minutes

1. **Create your account** - Sign up at [comply.coralledger.com](https://comply.coralledger.com)
2. **Set up your business** - Enter your TIN and business details
3. **Import transactions** - Upload your CSV or connect Point POS
4. **Review categorization** - Verify VAT categories are correct
5. **Generate your return** - Create your VAT return with one click
