# Set Up Your Business

Configure your business profile to start managing VAT compliance.

## Required Information

- **Business Name** - Your registered business name
- **Trading Name** - Name you operate under (if different)
- **TIN** - Tax Identification Number from the Comptroller
- **VAT Number** - Your VAT registration number
- **Business Address** - Physical location in The Bahamas

## Optional Settings

- **Industry** - Helps with transaction categorization
- **Food Store License** - Required for 5% reduced rate eligibility
- **Logo** - For branded VAT invoices

## VAT Registration

:::info VAT Threshold
Businesses with taxable supplies over $100,000 BSD annually must register for VAT.
:::

If you're not yet registered:
1. Apply at the Department of Inland Revenue
2. Receive your VAT number
3. Enter it in CoralLedger Comply
