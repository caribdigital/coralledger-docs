# Cash Flow Report

The cash flow report helps you understand your VAT payment obligations, track upcoming deadlines, and review historical payments.

## Accessing the Report

Navigate to **Reports > Cash Flow**.

## Summary Cards

The report displays four key metrics:
- **Total Outstanding** — Unpaid VAT obligations across all periods
- **Due Next 30 Days** — VAT payments due within the next month
- **Paid Last 90 Days** — Total VAT paid in the past quarter
- **Overdue Amount** — Payments past their due date

## Payment Timeline

A visual timeline shows your payment obligations over the next 90 days:
- Upcoming payments shown as timeline markers
- **Overdue** payments highlighted with warning badges
- Click any payment to view details

## Report Sections

### Upcoming Obligations
The top 5 upcoming VAT payment deadlines, showing:
- Filing period
- Due date
- Amount owed

### Recent Payments
The last 5 VAT payments made, including:
- Payment date
- Amount paid
- Payment method

### Monthly Summary
A 6-month history showing:
- Month
- Number of payments made
- Total amount paid
- Returns settled

## Exporting

Click **Export CSV** to download the full cash flow breakdown including all obligations, payments, and monthly summaries.

## Empty State

If no VAT returns have been filed yet, the report will display guidance on how to get started with filing.
