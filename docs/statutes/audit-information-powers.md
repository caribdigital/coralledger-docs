# Audit and Information Powers

## What statute says

Tax authorities may request records, explanations, and supporting evidence to verify VAT compliance. Businesses are expected to provide complete, timely, and coherent documentation of filed returns and underlying transactions.

The practical standard is audit readiness: records should be traceable from source document to return line, with a clear history of adjustments and user actions.

## What platform does

CoralLedger Comply provides immutable-style audit logging, report export options, and event-level visibility across transaction, return, and administrative actions. This helps teams assemble audit packs and demonstrate control history.

Cross-functional views in Audit Trail and related reports support faster issue investigation when regulators request transaction-level detail.

## Customer responsibility

You remain responsible for responding to official information requests, confirming legal privilege boundaries, and providing complete source evidence outside the platform where required.

Customer teams should maintain an audit response process that includes ownership, timelines, and document quality checks.
