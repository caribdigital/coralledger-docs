## Quick Facts

### VAT Registration Threshold
**$100,000 BSD** annual taxable supplies

### Standard VAT Rate
**10%** on most goods and services

### Filing Deadlines
**21 days** after the end of the tax period (standard); **14 days** for large taxpayers (annual turnover ≥ $5M)

### Record Retention
**7 years** minimum
